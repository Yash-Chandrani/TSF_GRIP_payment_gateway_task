Our Story

Since 2008, the Sparks Foundation has been dedicated to providing emergency relief to those most in need. We've helped over 4 million people in 20+ countries through our network of partners.

Mission, Vision

Sparks Foundation is a nonprofit organization that provides aid and assistance to those in need through funding, volunteers, and donations. We work with local partners to provide the help and resources that families need in order to live with dignity.

Help us help others

Supported by private donations alone, we achieve what governments can't do alone. With your support, we are able to continue our work of providing hope for those who have lost any reason to dream.

Donate now to make a difference

Donating is a powerful way to make a difference. By donating, you are making it possible for others who are less fortunate than you to be able to enjoy all of the same things in life that you take for granted. You're contributing to the well-being of your own community by giving others the opportunity for a better future.
